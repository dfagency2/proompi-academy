## What is Proompi?

Proompi is an AI-powered creative platform designed for businesses and professionals who need high-quality visual, audio, and text content at scale. Unlike consumer-focused AI tools that offer limited models and one-off generations, Proompi gives you access to the world's best AI models in one unified workspace, with powerful automation capabilities that transform how your team creates content.

Think of Proompi as your creative AI team that works 24/7. Whether you're generating product images for an e-commerce catalog, creating social media videos, producing background music for presentations, or building automated content pipelines, Proompi handles it all without requiring technical skills or API knowledge.

## What You Can Create

### Images
Generate photorealistic product shots, marketing visuals, social media graphics, and artistic compositions using state-of-the-art models like GPT-image, FLUX, and Ideogram. Each model excels at different tasks: GPT-image for commercial photorealism, FLUX for artistic textures, and Ideogram for images that include text or typography.

### Video
Create professional video content with Sora 2, Luma Ray 2, Kling v1.6, and Wan 2.1. From product demonstrations to brand hero videos, our video models handle everything from smooth camera movements to complex scene transitions. You can start from text descriptions or use existing images as starting points.

### Music and Audio
Generate royalty-free background music and soundscapes with ElevenLabs integration. Perfect for video content, presentations, podcasts, or brand ambiance. Specify mood, genre, and duration up to 10 minutes.

### Text Content
Produce marketing copy, product descriptions, blog posts, social media captions, and more using Claude Haiku and GPT-4o-mini. The text generation nodes integrate seamlessly with visual workflows, allowing you to generate captions alongside images automatically.

### Automated Workflows
This is where Proompi becomes transformative. The visual Workflow Builder lets you connect 11 different node types to create repeatable content pipelines. Imagine scraping your competitor's website, extracting their color palette, and automatically generating 20 brand-aligned social posts in their style. Or building a product catalog generator that takes product names and creates professional photos with optimized descriptions. These workflows save hours of repetitive work and ensure consistency across all your content.

## Your First Generation in 4 Steps

Let's create your first AI image right now. This process takes less than 2 minutes:

### Step 1: Write Your Description
Navigate to the Image Generation page and type what you want to create. Start simple: "modern office workspace with laptop and coffee" or "minimalist product packaging for organic tea." Don't worry about making it perfect — that's what the next step handles.

### Step 2: Choose Enhancement Mode
Click the "Enhance Prompt" button and select one of three modes:

- **Quick**: Minimal improvements while preserving your original intent. Your "coffee on table" stays exactly that, just with better technical parameters.
- **Detailed**: Transforms your basic description into professional photography language. "Coffee on table" becomes "artisan espresso in white ceramic cup on aged oak table, soft morning light from left, bokeh background, commercial food photography, Canon 5D, f/2.8."
- **Creative**: Takes artistic liberty to create unexpected interpretations of your concept.

For your first try, use Detailed mode. It costs 2-3 credits and dramatically improves results.

### Step 3: Select Your Model
Choose an AI model based on your use case. For commercial-quality results, select **gpt-image-1** (15 credits). If you're just testing ideas, **flux-schnell** (2 credits) is perfect for rapid iteration. The model selector shows cost and quality ratings to help you decide.

### Step 4: Generate and Download
Click "Generate" and wait 10-30 seconds depending on the model. Once your images appear, click any thumbnail to view it full-size, then use the download button to save it. Every generation is automatically saved to your Gallery for future reference.
